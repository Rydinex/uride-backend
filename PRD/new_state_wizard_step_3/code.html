Non-Discrimination Policy

Required State-Specific Language

As per California Civil Code section 51, Sovereign and its platform partners maintain a zero-tolerance policy regarding discrimination of any kind.

Platform users are prohibited from discriminating against riders or drivers based on:

  • Race, color, or national origin
  • Religion or creed
  • Physical or mental disability
  • Medical condition or genetic information
  • Marital status or sexual orientation
  • Age (40 and over)

Note: Any violation of this policy must be reported to the California Department of Fair Employment and Housing (DFEH) within 48 hours of occurrence.
